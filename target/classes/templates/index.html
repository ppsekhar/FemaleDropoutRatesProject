Girls` education is a primary predictor for national productivity and family income. Yet, in many parts of the world including Africa, the Middle-East and East Asia, the the female dropout rate is alarming. This ongoing project aims to connect educators and activists with accurate information in the cloud to help women around the world finish their education

 

My goal is to bring the gravity of the situation into global focus by continuously gathering information related to girls dropping out and provide trends, events and issues causing the dropouts.
